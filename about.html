Portal: A Influencial Classic

Portal is a 2007 puzzle-platform game developed and published by Valve.
Influencial due to a ground breaking mechanic. The ablity to seamlessly tavel between two flat planes.

Why Portal?

It's fun! The hilarious commentary and great atmosphere pulls the game together, but using portals to solve puzzles is endlessly fun.

Basic Info

The game begins with the player waking up in a seemingly normal laboratory.
However, the player begins to notice that the voice speaking to her may not be automated and no-one seems to be watching her through the obersvation windows.
She needs to use the skills she gained traversing the deadly gaunlet against the AI to find out what happened to laboratory and hopefully get out alive.
