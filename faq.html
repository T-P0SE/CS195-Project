How much does Portal cost?

Portal costs $10.00. Though sales are extremely frequent, occasionally bringing Portal as low as .99 cents!

My computer isn't steller. Can I player Portal?

Likely Yes! Portal is not a incredibly straining game so even older computers shouldn't struggle.
Here's the recommeded specs incase you're still not sure:
Pentium 4 processor (3.0GHz, or better), 1GB RAM, DirectX® 9 level Graphics Card, Windows® 7 (32/64-bit)/Vista/XP, Mouse, Keyboard, Internet Connection

Is Portal appropriate for kids?

Yes! Portal is rated Teen by the ESRB which means Portal is suited for anyone 13 years and older.

Does Portal have achievements?

Yes! Portal contains 15 achievements you can unlock anytime!

Is Portal a linear game?

Yes. Though where you start and end is Linear, how you traverse the levels and solve the puzzles is completely up to you!

Do I get enough bang for my buck? What's included with Portal?

Portal contains the main storyline, plus the entire main story with Developer commentary which gives insights on how Portal was made by the developers themselves.
As well as many challenge modes which allow you to test your skills with limited portals and limited time!

Is Portal a difficult game?

In some places, yes! Portal requires you to think about the solution, but it should never be frusturating.

Does portal have a story?

Yes! Though information isn't given to the player in a traditional way. There is absolutely a story to be found!

How long is Portal?

Portals main story can be beaten in three hours. But there is plenty of other content included in the game to explore aswell!

Alright that sounds really good! But when I'm finished with Portal then what?

You can move on to Portal 2!
